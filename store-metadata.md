## Description (4000 characters max)
LittleLearner is a fun and engaging learning app designed to help young children build essential skills through colorful, interactive activities.

**Activities:**
🎨 Colors — Learn and identify colors with bright visuals
🔤 Letters — Recognize letters of the alphabet
🔢 Numbers — Count and learn numbers
🔷 Shapes — Identify basic shapes
🐾 Animals — Discover animals and their names
😊 Emotions — Understand and recognize emotions

**Features:**
⭐ Stars and rewards to motivate learning
🔊 Sound effects for every interaction
🎉 Celebration animations when activities are completed
👨‍👩‍👧 Parent account to track your child's progress
📵 No ads — completely ad-free experience
🔒 Child-safe — no personal data collected from children

LittleLearner is perfect for children ages 2–7 and is especially helpful for children who benefit from visual and structured learning.

All activities are designed to be simple, engaging, and rewarding. Each correct answer earns a star, and completing an activity triggers a fun celebration!
